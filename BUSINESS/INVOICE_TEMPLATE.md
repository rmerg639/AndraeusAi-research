## Invoice Numbering System

Format: `ANDRAI-YYYY-NNN`

Examples:
- ANDRAI-2025-001 (First invoice)
- ANDRAI-2025-002 (Second invoice)
- ANDRAI-2026-001 (First invoice of 2026)
